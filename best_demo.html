All About It

Best is a company made by three people. This was made to help people publish books, sell things, and give some of it to people in need! We appreciate donations!

So, this started on September 2023 when two friends decided to make a company about books because from that we can educate people. Later another friend came and said, "Let's also make games to expand the company."

But the problem started from there! We did not focus on books, which led to fights. First of all, we did not know what to name the company. One friend said, "Just name it VEA," which led to arguments.

Then we all decided to choose the name BEST. And that is how Best was made. Best stands for Books, Electronics, Savers, and Toys.
